Crop Calendar

Kharif Season (June to September):

Major Crops:

Paddy (Rice):

The dominant crop in Dakshina Kannada due to its coastal location and abundant monsoon rains.

Parecanut (Betel nut):

A commercially important crop grown extensively in the region.

Other Crops:

Coconut:

A major plantation crop in Dakshina Kannada.

Banana:

Another important plantation crop.

Vegetables like okra, gourds, eggplant, and leafy greens. Spices like ginger and turmeric (grown on a smaller scale).

Rabi Season (October to March):

Major Crops:

Vegetables like tomato, capsicum, beans, and peas. Some short-duration pulses like green gram and black gram.

Zaid Season (April to May):

Limited Cultivation:

Due to the hot and dry weather, cultivation is limited in the Zaid season.
