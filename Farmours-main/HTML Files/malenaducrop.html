Crop Calendar

Major Crops:

Paddy (Rice):

Grown in valleys and plains with adequate water availability. Some areas might cultivate red rice varieties.

Ragi (Finger millet):

A drought-tolerant staple crop well-suited to Malenadu's hilly terrain.

Finger millet and other Millets (like Jowar and Kodo):

Gaining popularity due to their nutritional value and suitability for the region's climate.

Other Crops:

Plantation crops like Arecanut (Betel nut) and Coconut in suitable areas.

Vegetables like beans, cucumber, and leafy greens adapted to the monsoon season.

Spices like cardamom, pepper, and clove on a smaller scale, particularly in Kerala's Malenadu region.

Rabi Season (October to March):

Major Crops:

Vegetables like potato, cauliflower, cabbage, and carrots that thrive in the cooler winter temperatures.

Some short-duration pulses like xgreen gram and black gram.

Coffee:

A major plantation crop in some parts of Malenadu, particularly in Karnataka and Kerala.

Zaid Season (April to May):

Limited Cultivation:

Similar to Dakshina Kannada, cultivation is limited due to hot and dry weather.
